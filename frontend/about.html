Our Story

About K&A Fragrances

We are passionate fragrance curators dedicated to helping you discover your signature scent — without the risk of blind buying.

Our mission is to provide you with the finest selection of authentic fragrances — from rich Middle Eastern ouds and iconic designer bottles to rare niche artisan perfumes — available in sample sizes from just 2ml, so you can explore without committing.

Discover exclusive scents in tiny treasures.

Our Vision

At K&A Fragrances, we are more than a fragrance retailer. We are curators of sensory experiences — experts in the art of scent and advocates for thoughtful consumption. Our vision is to empower every customer to make confident fragrance decisions by offering them the chance to experience a scent before they invest in a full bottle.

Why Decants & Samples?

We understand the frustration of spending on a full-sized fragrance only to find it's not quite right. That's why every fragrance in our catalog is available as a 2ml, 5ml, or 10ml decant — at a fraction of the full-bottle price.

  • Save Your MoneyTry before you commit — avoid the costly mistake of blind buying a full bottle.
  • Explore MoreExperience a wide range of scents from the comfort of your home, with no pressure to choose just one.
  • Shop MindfullyEncourage intentional purchases — invest only in fragrances you genuinely love.

Our Passion

With a deep passion for the art of fragrance, K&A Fragrances carefully selects each scent in our catalog — ensuring you receive only the best and most sought-after fragrances in the world. Every item we carry is 100% authentic, sourced from verified suppliers. We take pride in our Authenticity Guarantee and back it on every single order.
